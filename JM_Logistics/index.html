Our Process

From First Call to Final Delivery

Getting started is simple. Our four-step dispatch process takes you from onboarding to your first profitable load — and builds a long-term partnership that keeps your trucks full.

01

Get Started

Share your MC/DOT info, equipment type, home base, and preferred lanes. We'll review your setup and assign a dedicated dispatcher who fits your operation.

02

We Find Loads

Your dispatcher searches load boards and broker networks, vets each opportunity, and negotiates rates — presenting only loads that meet your standards and revenue targets.

03

You Drive

Once you approve a load, we send rate confirmations, manage broker communication, track your trip, and handle any issues that come up — you just drive.

04

Deliver & Repeat

After delivery, we assist with POD submission and invoicing. Then we immediately start sourcing your next load — building a consistent freight pipeline for your business.
